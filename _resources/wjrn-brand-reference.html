Surface Notes
Production usage: the header wordmark on the live site is always rendered white via filter: brightness(0) invert(1) — the dark mark exists as a source asset for light-background contexts (print, partner placements) rather than in-app use.
Station backgrounds: each station page uses a dark radial mesh gradient tinted toward its accent (e.g. Rock Garden: #222b15 → #121809) rather than a flat surface color — the accent shows up as glow and light, not fill.
Homepage ground: #050201 — near-black, warm rather than neutral, consistent across all pages before per-station tinting is layered in.
